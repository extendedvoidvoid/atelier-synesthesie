### Directives for Hermes:
*   **The Forms:** Feed the links from `ROADMAP.md` into Hermes (e.g., [BytePlus vStart](https://www.byteplus.com/en/activity/vstart), [Microsoft Founders Hub](https://portal.startups.microsoft.com/signup)).

## ⚙️ Phase 2: Orchestrating the Video Printing Press (Q3 2026)

Once admitted and credits are secured, Hermes moves from "application assistant" to your **Live Production Orchestrator**, driving the `od` CLI under Open Design to manage your channel network.

### Directives for Hermes:
*   **Sourcing Orchestration:** Hermes runs automated cron commands or daemon triggers:
    ```bash
    od project sync --source apple-music-charts
    ```
    This triggers your sourcing agent to mine the top albums and check for verified motion cover art.
*   **Transient-Driven Render Campaigns:** Hermes drives the compositor:
    ```bash
    od media generate --prompt-file prompt.txt --aspect 9:16 --output campaign_01.mp4
    ```
    It feeds the transient beat-sync parameters and dynamic word bounds to your local M3 Max HEVC compiler.
*   **Dynamic Channel Printing:** A single successful master compile is automatically localized by Hermes across **7 channels simultaneously**, managing your multi-language video outputs on autopilot.

### Directives for Hermes:
*   **The Subtitle Verification Loop:** Hermes scans generated ASS subtitle tracks to ensure 100% clean typographical glyphs, auto-flagging and stripping any smart-quote syntax anomalies before the final compile.
*   **Canal+ Signature Checks:** Hermes verifies that the white-boxed `"CESAR+"` watermark is overlayed perfectly at the top-left coordinate boundary (`x=60, y=160`) across all 7 localized variants.
*   **Monthly Disk Cleansing:** Since local M3 Max processing creates large temp scraps under `work_0xx/` folders, Hermes runs a automated command to flush out temporary folders, maintaining a zero-leak disk state.
