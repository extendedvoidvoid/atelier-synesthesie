## 🚀 Step 1: The Master Copilot Prompt

Open PowerPoint, click the **Copilot** button on the top ribbon, and paste this exact prompt:

```text
Create a 12-slide presentation about "CraftCut", a premium French creative-tech startup developed at Station F, Paris. 

Use an editorial minimalist style. Focus on extreme high-contrast, generous margins, and an off-white background (#FBFBFA) paired with off-black typography (#1A1A19). Utilize 'Instrument Serif' and 'Switzer' fonts.

Ensure the presentation includes:
1. Title: CraftCut (César+ Start-up)
2. Executive Summary: Transforming music art, fashion layouts, and the noble legacy of 'Les Ouvriers de France' into vertical video essays.
3. The Problem: The rise of generic 'AI slop' that dilutes brand prestige.
4. The Solution: Our proprietary "CraftCut" pipeline for editorial video creation at the forefront of content creation.
5. Sourcing Agent: Automatic Apple Music and design-register motion mining.
6. Localization Agent: Dynamic Word-Bounding algorithms ensuring videos stay strictly under 60 seconds across 7 languages (German at 85 words, Romance at 120 words).
7. Typographic Synthesizer: SSA/ASS styling, clean margins, and the white-boxed "CESAR+" broadcast signature overlay.
8. Hardware Performance: Local-first, zero-cost rendering on Apple M3 Max GPUs.
9. Token Scaling: Why we consume 64.3M tokens/month, requiring enterprise cloud APIs.
```

## 🖌️ Step 2: Refining Layouts with PowerPoint "Designer"

Once Copilot generates the base slides, use PowerPoint's native **Designer** tool (located in the *Home* tab) to apply the 2026 editorial aesthetic:

1.  **Warm Off-White Background:**
    *   If Copilot uses standard stark white, go to `Design -> Format Background` and set the color to hex `#FBFBFA`. This warmer canvas instantly makes your presentation look premium and editorial.
2.  **Typography Pairings:**
    *   Set your display headers to **Instrument Serif** (available on Microsoft Cloud fonts or Google Fonts) with a slight letter-spacing reduction.
    *   Set body fonts to **Switzer** or **SF Pro Display** for an ultra-clean, Swiss-grotesque feel.
3.  **Bento & Grid Layouts:**
    *   For the Sourcing, Localization, and Typography slides, use the **Designer** panel to select grid/bento-box card layouts with thin **1px borders** and generous breathing room.

## 🎥 Step 4: Video Previews (M3 Max Power)

Once your deck is complete:
1.  Go to `File -> Export -> Create a Video`.
2.  Select **Ultra HD (4K)**.
3.  Set slide duration to **5.0 seconds**.
4.  Export. On your **M3 Max**, PowerPoint will utilize hardware-accelerated HEVC pipelines, rendering a gorgeous, cinema-ready presentation video in seconds. This is perfect to play during your Station F pitch or upload to your GitHub website!
